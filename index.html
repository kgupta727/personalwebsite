About me



Hi! I’m Kavya, a computer science student at the University of Alabama in Tuscaloosa. Originally from India, I came to the United States to dive deeper into technology and innovation, and the journey has been both exciting and challenging in the best ways. Since moving here, I’ve been balancing academics, friendships, and exploring a new culture—whether it’s cheering at Bryant-Denny Stadium or discovering hidden spots around Tuscaloosa with friends.

Music is a big part of who I am. I’ve played the guitar for years, and it’s one of my favorite ways to express myself. Whether I’m learning new songs, experimenting with different styles, or simply unwinding with familiar chords, playing guitar has been a constant source of joy and creativity. It’s helped me stay grounded and find balance, especially when life gets busy.

On the academic side, I’m passionate about using technology to make a difference, especially in healthcare. I’ve been fortunate enough to win some awards for my projects and research, which encourages me to keep exploring, learning, and refining my skills. I love participating in hackathons and competitions, where I get to connect with others who share my enthusiasm for problem-solving and collaboration.

Beyond that, I’m always up for a casually-competitive sporting session and I hit the gym regularly to stay energized and focused. I’m someone who values teamwork, kindness, and the chance to contribute to a better world through tech. Thanks for stopping by—I’d love to connect!
